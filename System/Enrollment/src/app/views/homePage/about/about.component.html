Historical Background of Sta. Cruz Learning Center

The school started its operation in June 2001 for the school year 2001-2002, bearing the name STA. CRUZ LEARNING CENTER, named after the Patron Saint of the barangay where this school is located.

On its first year of operations, a two-room makeshift structure was utilized for the kindergarten classes composed of 45 kids each...

The first batch of First Year High School class of this school was composed of 7 students – 4 boys and 3 girls – handled by a licensed BSEd graduate. The 5 of them – 4 boys and 1 girl, were confirmed graduation on March 31, 2008.

Government recognition for Pre-school and Elementary Grades Programs was granted on August 5, 2008, followed by Secondary Level recognition on August 8, 2008...

After 2 unsuccessful attempts in 2011-2012, this school now waves a proud FAPE Certification, having passed evaluation on November 23, 2016.

Today, SCLC boasts a well-equipped campus with facilities including a library, Computer Lab, Audio-Visual Room, and Cultural Activity Center, and is staffed by dedicated faculty and administrators.
